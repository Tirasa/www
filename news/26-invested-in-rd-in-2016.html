    26% invested in R&S in 2016

    28 Dec 2016
    During 2016 Tirasa has invested about 26% of its production capacity on Apache Syncope and ConnId. A point more than in 2015.

    Since the beginning Tirasa decided to mainly focus on the Identity Management and Access Control.

    The investment in this direction has always been substantial and continuous; starting in 2011 when the Identity Management platform Syncope was donated to the Apache Software Foundation (ASF) becoming Apache Syncope.

    As is well known, Tirasa continues to lead the project and its development: during 2015 Tirasa has invested about 25% of its production capacity on Apache Syncope and ConnId. In the current year that is going to finish we managed to do something more by reaching the threshold of 26%.

    Several new features have been included by Tirasa directly. Many other interesting activities are planned for the next future; the 2017 will be really interesting and we are ready to give our best.

     

    But how could a small company like Tirasa invest so much in research and development without the help of those who continue to trust our expertise?

    All of them, that by purchasing our services allow us to live our dream and to pursue our ideas, definitely deserve our thanks.

     

    If not yet done, please follow us on Twitter and LinkedIn and help us to improve our services.

    Further, help Apache Syncope project, share your reference-case.
