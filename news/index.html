    Apache Syncope

    We designed Syncope because we are IdM experts and Open Source enthusiasts. The idea was to create an agile Open Source Identity Manager, lightweight and incisive, able to meet the requirements of enterprise environments.

    We started Tirasa with the goal of accelerating the development and diffusion of Syncope IdM.

    In February 2012 we donated Syncope IdM to The Apache Software Foundation.
