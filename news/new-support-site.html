    New support site available

    14 May 2012
    Looking for enterprise support information about Apache Syncope?

    Looking for enterprise support information about Apache Syncope, the Open Source Identity Manager by Apache? New support site for Apache Syncope is available today at http://www.tirasa.net/businessproposition/apache-syncope.html.

    After Syncope joining the Apache Software Foundation, Tirasa - an Open Source company providing value add support and professional services around Apache Syncope - has redesigned and published a new support website completely dedicated to Apache Syncope.

    This enterprise support site is itself a concentrate of Open Source technologies; content is

    1. managed by Hippo CMS
    2. published by Apache Cocoon 3.0
    3. delivered by OpenShift

    Take a look at http://www.tirasa.net/businessproposition/apache-syncope.html for more information.
